Burton Kids' Burke Hood Neck Gaiter

Keep your child warm and dry during outdoor play with the Burton Kids' Burke Hood Neck Gaiter. Designed with DRYRIDE technology, this neck gaiter offers breathability and quick-drying power, making it the perfect companion for winter adventures. The stretchy, soft fabric ensures a comfortable fit that moves with your child, while the anti-pilling construction keeps the gaiter looking fresh and soft for longer.
